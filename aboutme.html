HI! My name is Anna gurgenidze, and I am from Georgia, Tbilisi. I got my start as a graphic designer by creating a logo for my dad's paper company. As my friends began to like my work, I began to design materials for everything and everyone. Of course, I was not good in brand and graphic design at first, that is why I decided to learn by myself to be much better than I was earlier, I also went to multiple academies and got certified. Today, I am open to all kinds of design work, although I still have a lot to learn, but I am sure you will like my designs.

When I'm not working on my latest graphic design project, I might be thrift shopping, cuddling with my pet rabbit or Gaming with my friends. I am fully invested in my community and would love to work or volunteer to gain experience with a brand that shares these values.

If you like my work, please contact me. I can't wait to learn about your brand's design needs!
